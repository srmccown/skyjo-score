Skyjo Scoring, Quick Reference

  • Each player starts with 12 face-down cards in a 4×3 grid and flips 2 face up.
  • On a turn, draw or take the discard, then swap it into your grid or discard and flip one card.
  • If a whole column (3 cards) is face up and matching, it's cleared and counts as 0.
  • A round ends when a player reveals all 12 cards; everyone else gets one final turn, then all hands are revealed.
  • Each player's round score is the sum of their visible cards (cleared columns = 0).
  • Penalty: if the player who ended the round doesn't have the strictly lowest score that round, their score is doubled.
  • Round scores add up over the game. Once any player reaches 100+, the round after finishes and the lowest total wins.
